What's New With Background App Management on Z Fold 8

Here's something most foldable reviews miss: how does the phone handle background apps across its two screens? The Z Fold 8 is expected to ship with significant improvements to how One UI 8 manages multitasking memory — a major pain point on Z Fold 6 where aggressive background app killing would interrupt work when switching between cover and inner display.

Samsung is working on what insiders call "Continuity Mode" — a feature where the app state is fully preserved and instantly resumed regardless of which screen you're using. This directly addresses one of the most common complaints from Z Fold users who rely on productivity apps like Notion, Gmail, or Slack across both displays.

💡 Optimization Tip

On current Z Fold devices, you can reduce app killing by going to Settings → Device Care → Battery → Background Usage Limits and removing productivity apps from the restricted list. This will carry over to the Z Fold 8 and is even more critical given its larger app surface area.

Galaxy Z Fold 8 vs. Z Fold 6: Is It Worth Upgrading?

If you're rocking the Z Fold 6, the question is whether the Z Fold 8 justifies the spend. The short answer: it depends on what bothers you most. If the crease annoys you daily — yes, upgrade. If you want thinner form factor for pockets — absolutely. If you're happy with your camera and performance — maybe skip and wait for the Z Fold 9.

✅
Upgrade If...
You use the inner display for productivity daily, want the creaseless panel, or need the new 200MP camera system.
⏳
Wait If...
You bought the Z Fold 6 within the last 8 months. The generational leap is meaningful but not urgent for recent buyers.
🆕
Buy If New...
First time going foldable? The Z Fold 8 is the most refined entry point Samsung has ever offered at this tier.

Launch Date, Colors, and Where to Buy

Samsung's Unpacked event is expected in the third week of July 2026, with pre-orders opening immediately after. The Z Fold 8 is expected to ship in Phantom Black, Icy Blue, and a new Sand Gold colorway exclusive to Samsung.com. Carrier availability will be global from day one.
